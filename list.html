Description

Remuera has its favourite positions. Those who now live there count themselves lucky.

Perched high on the ridge below Kings School and Remuera Road, this cavity-clad (in 2007) family home offers much more than meets the eye from the street.

Not only does it have four bedrooms, there's also a handy office by the front door and a double internal access garage.

The real surprise is the amazing easy-care courtyard. Flowing directly from the kitchen/family area, it's an ideal spot for entertaining with sublime privacy and great sun. You'll spend countless hours relaxing here.

Ideal as a lock and leave city base in DGZ and near all the best schools, bedrooms afford great separation and soothing views from the upper level.

Available for the first time since 2013, this brilliant home offers astute purchasers a chance to pick up a great buy in a superbly elevated and sought-after northern slopes location.
