Description

Auction: 5:30pm, Sunday 5 March 2023 on site (unless sold prior) Open Home: Sunday 1:30pm - 2:00pm or by appointment

Conjunctionals are welcome.

This superbly well-appointed home on an elevated corner with views of Mt Wellington sits in heartland Stonefields and is often admired - and justifiably so. Well orientated to the northern sun and enjoying a rare, sizable 503 sqm (approx.) section for the children to play outdoors on, this exceptional 2010 built residence will appeal to growing families who may comprise elderly relatives, a nanny, or privacy-seeking adult kids still living at home.

The 203 sqm (approx.) interior layout benefits from the inclusion of a smart ventilation system, six heat pumps, and underfloor heating in bathrooms guaranteeing a warm and cosy winter. Open plan living and dining areas are anchored by sleek polished granite underfloor heated tiling, and this connects to the northeast facing patio if you fancy your mealtimes alfresco. The adjacent kitchen has been thoughtfully designed to include loads of cabinet storage, ample composite stone benchtops and dazzling stainless steel appliances. A second more formal separate lounge is a lovely retreat for the adults with built in gas fireplace and adjoins the entrance porch and spacious foyer. The slumber regions have been designed to either give parents secluded downstairs quarters with bathroom or more aptly would accommodate a dependent relative, for example. Upstairs the other master bedroom comprises charming balcony, walk in wardrobe and white fully tiled ensuite - a theme echoed in all the other bathrooms including family bathroom with bathtub and separate shower.

Double internal access garaging also has a heat pump and includes a laundry while there's off-street parking for two more vehicles. The neighbourhood is famous in Auckland for its community spirit and supremely easy access to a catalogue of amenities. The high decile Stonefields School is a short walk away as are shops and parks.
